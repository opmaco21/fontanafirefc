⚽ Registration & Fees

The registration fee is $200 per player. Families registering multiple children may request flexible payment options.

  • $45 league registration fee
  • First month’s fee ($30)
  • Practice jersey
  • Backpack with player number and team logo
  • Game uniform (jersey, shorts, socks)

Yes. A $30 monthly fee is due on or before the 5th of each month.

We accept players born between 2012 and 2021 (approximately ages 5–14).
