Registration & Fees

The registration fee is $200.00.

The registration fee includes:

  • First month’s fee ($30)
  • Practice jersey
  • Backpack with player number and team logo
  • Complete game-day uniform (jersey, shorts, and socks)

Yes. After the first month, a $30 monthly fee is due on or before the 5th of each month.

Practice Expectations

  • Blue or red practice jersey
  • Black shorts
  • Black socks
  • Shinguards
  • Soccer cleats
  • Soccer ball

Players are expected to arrive on time, prepared, and ready to listen and learn.

Game Day Requirements

  • Complete game uniform
  • Shinguards
  • Soccer cleats
  • Backpack

Important: A second jersey must be kept in the player’s backpack at all times.

Players must arrive at least 45 minutes before game time for warm-ups and preparation.

Communication

Band is our primary form of communication. All parents are required to join Band to receive schedules, updates, and announcements.

Game Schedule

Games are typically held on Saturdays and Sundays.

Game information is usually sent on Thursday or Friday before the weekend.
