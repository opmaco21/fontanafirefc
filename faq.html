Practice Expectations

Players must arrive on time and prepared with the following items:

  • Blue or red practice jersey
  • Black shorts
  • Black socks
  • Shinguards
  • Soccer cleats
  • Soccer ball

Players should come ready to listen, learn, and give their best effort.
