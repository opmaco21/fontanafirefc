Meet the Coach

Alfredo Gaspar

Head Coach · Fontana Fire FC

I am Alfredo Gaspar, Head Coach of Fontana Fire FC in Fontana, California. Soccer has been a part of my life since the age of three, and I have spent the last four years coaching young athletes.

I work with boys and girls ages 6 through 12, focusing on building confidence, skills, and a love for the game. My goal is to motivate each player while creating a positive and supportive team environment where children can grow both as athletes and individuals. I am committed to developing Fontana Fire FC into a competitive team within the SoCal Soccer League while helping every player reach their full potential.
